Bk Birla Centre For Education, is a Senior Secondary School (XI-XII), affiliated to Central Board of Secondary Education (CBSE). The School is a Boys Boarding School, with classes from VI to XII. It is an English Medium school. The school is located in Talegaon Dabhade. BK Birla Centre For Education was established in 1998. It is a Trust and is part of Sarala Birla Group of Schools. The school is member of Indian Public School Conference.
